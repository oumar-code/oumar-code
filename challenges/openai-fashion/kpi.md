# OpenAI Fashion — KPI Emphasis

## Most Important Metrics (in priority order)

1. **`quality_score`** — Primary KPI. Judges evaluate teaching quality and usefulness.
   Target: ≥ 0.90
2. **`reliability`** — Demo must not crash. Offline fallback always active.
   Target: ≥ 0.99
3. **`avg_latency_ms`** — Response speed matters for user experience.
   Target: ≤ 3000 ms (GPT-5.6 is slower but richer)
4. **`cost_per_run`** — Keep demo costs under control.
   Target: ≤ $0.05 per interaction

## Threshold Targets

| Metric | Minimum Passing | Gold Target |
|---|---|---|
| quality_score | ≥ 0.75 | ≥ 0.92 |
| reliability | ≥ 0.95 | ≥ 0.99 |
| avg_latency_ms | ≤ 5000 | ≤ 2500 |
| cost_per_run | ≤ $0.10 | ≤ $0.03 |

## Story Framing

Lead with **real problem + real users**: "We're teaching tailors in Lagos and Accra
skills that unlock a $300B industry — with AI that works even when the internet doesn't."

Then show the **Codex demo**: pattern_math.py functions were generated by Codex
side-by-side with GPT-5.6. Live recording is our strongest technical proof point.

Then show **skill progression**: a learner who started with 0 badges earning
"Zipper Pro" and "Dart Artist" in a single session.

## Offline Story

Demonstrate the offline queue:
1. Turn off internet in the demo
2. Submit 3 tutor questions — they queue locally
3. Turn internet back on — questions flush and resolve
4. Show the Service Worker cache serving the UI instantly even offline

This is our unique differentiator: no other Build Week team will have
a credible offline-first story for an AI app.
